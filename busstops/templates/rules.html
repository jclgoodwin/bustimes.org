Vehicle editing rules

Vehicle details (type, colour etc.) are user-editable. If you want to get involved, great! But please follow these rules, so you don’t waste your time and others’:

  1. If a vehicle’s tracking as the wrong vehicle (i.e. the “ticket machine code” is wrong), please don’t try to correct it on bustimes.org – instead, wait for the operator to correct their data.
  2. Don’t “remove from list” just because a vehicle’s been advertised for sale, was involved in an accident, or hasn’t tracked for a while.
  3. Don’t update a vehicle’s livery until it’s finished being repainted and you can share some evidence.
  4. Edit sparingly – you don’t need to “tidy up” or make everything “consistent”.

  5. Do use the “summary” field to link to some evidence, such as a picture you’ve uploaded to Imgur. But please don’t link to private Facebook groups – private means we can’t see them.

  6. Do not edit to leave messages for other users or report mistracks.

Users can report content which they believe to be harmful, illegal, or or otherwise unsuitable. If we deem your content to be harmful, illegal, or otherwise unsuitable, we will permanently delete that content, and may ban your account from adding or editing content.

FAQ

How do I add a vehicle?

You can’t – the lists on bustimes.org are just a by-product of the live bus tracker map. Other websites like buslists.uk are the future, and will always be more comprehensive.

Can I use this data?

Yes, it’s available in the API. Busmiles is an example of a site that uses it.

I have a different question, or maybe a comment

Ask me or the Discord server.
