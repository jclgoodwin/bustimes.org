Vehicle editing rules

Vehicle details (type, colour etc.) are user-editable. If you want to get involved, great! But please follow these rules, to avoid wasting your own and others' time:

  1. If a vehicle's tracking as the wrong vehicle (i.e. the "ticket machine code" is wrong), don't try to correct it on bustimes.org – just wait for the operator to correct the ticket machine.
  2. Don't "remove from list" just because a vehicle's been advertised for sale, was involved in an accident, or hasn't tracked for a while.
  3. Don't update a vehicle's livery until it's finished being repainted and you can share some evidence.

  4. Do use the "summary" field to link to some evidence, such as a picture you've uploaded to Imgur. But please don't link to private Facebook groups – private means we can't see them! (Screenshots are fine.)

  5. Don't use nonsense edits to try to "message" other users or "the admins". Instead, post in the Discord server or send me an email.

  6. "Armchair editing" is strongly discouraged. We have users from all around who know more about their local buses than you do.
  7. It's OK to give people the benefit of the doubt sometimes. Don't undo/decline someone else's changes unless you're sure they're wrong.

FAQ

Why was my edit disapproved?

To limit chaos, some users can approve/disapprove others' contributions. (This doesn't make them special or "admins" – there's no such thing!) I know some of the rules above are onerous, and they aren't written in stone, but if your edit was disapproved it probably broke one of them.

Why haven't you designed a perfect system for handling edits, voting, moderation etc?

Unfortunately it's hard, and a distraction from the main job of making a bus tracking and timetable website. It might be best to get rid of the whole thing, but it would be a shame to throw away everyone's collective work.

How do I add a vehicle?

You can't – the lists on bustimes.org are just a by-product of the live bus tracker map. Other websites like buslists.uk will always be more comprehensive.

How do I add or edit a livery?

Sorry, only a few users can do that, on the basis that too many cooks spoil the broth, and the livery icons don't need to be any more detailed than they already are.

Can I use this data?

Yes, it's available in the API. Busmiles is an example of a site that uses it.
